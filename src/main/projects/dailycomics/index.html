Hoshi's Daily Comics

I used to do this thing on Twitter where I posted nonsensical comics featuring my OC Hoshi and some of my friends (specifically one groupchat) everyday. Everybody hated them until I stopped posting them and suddenly I had 4 people ask me "where did your daily comics go ?!". So there was a Season 2. It didn't last long, it just wasn't fun anymore at some point. Then push came to shove, friend groups fell apart and the original spark of Hoshi's Daily Comics will probably never be recreated.
Nowadays, sometimes whenever I go on X, I get recommendations for similar style content. Quick comics made in MSPaint with a nonsensical punchline. And I remember just how fun is it to make something not even you understand.

Even if not that long lived, they were a decently important part of my online presence. It was, in a sense, what a few people knew me for. So I made an archive.
