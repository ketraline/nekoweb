    Patapon

    Patapon is a combination of the rhythm and RTS genre, in which you control an army of little eyeballs on their search for 'Earthend'. I'm still playing Patapon 3, but I also have Patapon 2.

    I have multiple Patapon OC's! Here's a picture of them celebrating Christmas. You can see more of them on my Toyhouse.

    Arknights

    Arknights is a mobile tower defense + gacha game. It's set in a post-apocalyptic future setting on the planet Terra. Originium, a valuable material, can infect people with a disease called oripathy, but also allows them to also use Arts magic. The ones suffering from oripathy are often shunned from society because of how infectious the disease is. Your role as the Doctor is to command your Operators to defend themselves from the Reunion movement and the multiple goverments of Terra, while in search for a cure.

    I have quite a beefy account! You can add me to friends, my username is Hoshi#6474.

    Lobotomy Corporation

    Lobotomy Corp falls into the management simulator genre. It is inspired by the SCP Foundation, Cabin in the Woods, and Warehouse 13. As the new manager at the company, your task is to watch over beings known as Abnormalities over the course of 50 days.

    The game is BRUTAL - it took me 200h to beat it, but at least I did it! I do have some OC's based on it, which you can see on my Toyhouse.
